How It Works

Hiring doesn't have to be complicated. Catena takes care of sourcing, screening, and shortlisting, so you can start with interview-ready candidates in just 72 hours.


01

Alignment

We refine your job description and define the ideal candidate profile based on your needs and hiring goals.

02

Search

We promote your role across the right channels, combining strategic sourcing, targeted advertising, and talent hunting.

03

Vetting

We assess applicants based on the role's criteria and deliver a curated shortlist of qualified candidates.

04

Candidates

You receive a focused list of pre-vetted candidates, ready for interviews—so you can hire faster with confidence.
